Key Words and what they mean:

  • "Symbol Undefined", "Unknown fucntion", "is not a key word":

    The function you called, key word you used, variable you refer to is not known. It usually means that there is a spelling error.

  • "Type mismatch":

    The computer was expecting one type of information and got another. It wanted a string and got a number or boolean.

  • "Illigal start":

    This almost always means you are missing a closing before the line the computer has a problem with.

  • "... is uninitilized." "... maybe uninitilized.":

    This you are trying to do a calculation or assigning a value with a variable that may not have a value yet.
